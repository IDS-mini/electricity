The Electricity Usage Optimizer - save on your electricity bills by planning your consumption!

Charing a car, drying laundry, heating your home? With hourly-rates swinging fiercely, some planning on your upcoming consumption can save you a lot of money. The Electricity Usage Optimizer helps you to plan your electricity consumption thus saving you from the worst-case bills.

If you can plan and shift your electricity needs within in the next days, we will show you the best times to use the electricity - and when to definitely turn the costly gadgets off.

You won't only save on your own bill. With your help, others will save too!

Cutting the total consumption during the peak hours may have an extensive effect on the prices (article in Finnish). When the electricity production is at its limits, every new unit of production costs more than the previous one. This increasing marginal cost phenomenon has an upside: it really is benefical to reduce peak-time consumption. Every unit of consumption saved during peak-hours drop the market price more than they would during off-peak. So by planning your consumption and dodging the peaks, you will save on your bills but your neighbor will save on theirs too.

Predictions during unusual times?

Simply put: yes. The current electricity prices may be at a level we are not used to see. But that does not make all correlations vanish. Routines in societies affect the total consumption: factories and industrial production run with their weekly routines. Temperature changes increase or decrease our demand for heating and windy days boost the wind power production.

So, there are still familiar and predictable components that give us useful insight on the coming days. And because the current prices can be a vast burden for households with considerable consumption and our society, these insights are now needed more than ever.
